ME, MYSELF AND I

PANDEMIC EDITION



At the start of this year I had secured a new job in VR Gaming.
I couldn't have been more excited.

Then, like most of the world, my plans were thrown into disarray with the arrival of COVID.
My new job offer was withdrawn.

In July my contract with NBC was terminated after 31/2 years.
As a PAYE Contractor I was ineligible for furlough.

I was unemployed. I was lost.

This year to date, I have applied for over 200 jobs.
I've redone my CV several times.

This process has definitely knocked my confidence
However, when life throws us a curveball we have to find new ways to dodge it.

I have wanted to learn to code for a long time, but had never been in a position to do so.

When I saw the note from my job coach for this course on my Job seekers diary.
I felt like it was fate.

So I applied. I was accepted and that is how I came to be a student with Code Nation.

I cannot tell you how grateful I have been for this opportunity and how nice
it has been to reconnect with other human beings after 9 months of being at home.

Especially after spending 5 of those months unemployed.
